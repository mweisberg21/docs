The Cake Day dashboard at [cakeday.io/app](https://cakeday.io/app) is your central control panel for everything outside of Slack. From here you can see upcoming celebrations, manage who's on your roster, configure channels and post timing, and handle billing — all in one place.

## Sign in

Go to [cakeday.io/app](https://cakeday.io/app) and click **Sign in with Slack**. You'll be redirected through Slack's OAuth flow and returned to the dashboard once authenticated. Only members of your installed Slack workspace can sign in.

## Dashboard sections

| Section | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Home** | An at-a-glance view of your workspace: a usage strip showing how many celebrations you've sent against your annual plan cap, and a list of upcoming birthdays and work anniversaries. |
| **Roster** | All workspace members who have joined, been invited, or been removed from Cake Day. Add individuals, import via CSV, and edit dates. |
| **Settings** | Configure celebration channels, post time, timezone, and the pause/resume toggle. Pro workspaces also manage AI voice and custom branding here. |
| **Admins** | View and manage which team members have Cake Day admin access. |
| **Billing** | See your current plan, usage, and upgrade or change your subscription. |

## Finding your annual usage

Your celebration usage (celebrations sent vs. your plan's annual cap) appears as a strip at the top of the Home page. You can also check it any time from Slack with `/cakeday usage`. Usage resets on the anniversary of your workspace's install date.
