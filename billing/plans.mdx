Cake Day bills by **celebrations per year**, not by how many people are on your roster. Every plan supports an unlimited number of workspace members; what changes between plans is how many shoutouts Cake Day can post in a 12-month window, plus which features are unlocked.

Annual billing saves 17% (two months free) on all paid plans. See the [official pricing page](https://cakeday.io/pricing) for the latest rates and to start a checkout.

## Plan details

### Free — $0

For small teams getting started. Cake Day posts up to **30 celebrations per year** with AI-generated messages and curated GIFs. You get one admin and one shared channel for both birthdays and anniversaries.

### Starter — $19/month · $190/year

For growing teams who want everyone celebrated. The cap rises to **150 celebrations per year**, and you can add up to **3 admins**. Starter also unlocks separate channels for birthdays and anniversaries, broadcast invites, CSV roster import, and 365-day activity history.

### Growth — $49/month · $490/year

For teams that want richer tools and more admins. Growth raises the cap to **500 celebrations per year** with **unlimited admins**, adds team leader roles, and lets you build a custom GIF library of up to **15 GIFs**. Activity history is retained indefinitely.

### Pro — $99/month · $990/year

For larger orgs that want every feature. Pro removes the celebration cap entirely (**unlimited**), adds **custom AI prompts** to set the tone for your team, **custom shoutout branding** (display name and icon), a custom GIF library of up to **50 GIFs**, and priority support in Slack.

<Note>
Annual billing saves 17% compared to paying month-by-month — that's two months free on every paid plan. You can switch between monthly and annual billing at any time from the Billing page in the dashboard.
</Note>
