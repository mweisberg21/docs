Cake Day automatically celebrates your teammates' birthdays and work anniversaries in Slack. Each day at the time you choose, it generates a personalized message via Google Gemini, pairs it with a curated GIF, and posts it in your celebration channel — tagging the person so the whole team can chime in.

## How it works

When you install Cake Day, you pick a celebration channel and a post time. From there, each teammate opts in by running `/cakeday me` in Slack and entering their own birthday and work start date. You never manage a spreadsheet or ask HR to export a roster — members add their own dates through a friendly modal, and admins see who's on the roster from the dashboard at [cakeday.io/app/roster](https://cakeday.io/app/roster).

## Pricing

Cake Day bills by celebrations per year — not by how many people are on your roster. Every plan lets you add unlimited teammates; you only pay more if more of them actually get celebrated.

| Plan | Price | Celebrations / year |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free | \$0 | 30 |
| Starter | \$19 / month | 150 |
| Growth | \$49 / month | 500 |
| Pro | \$99 / month | Unlimited |

When your workspace reaches 80% of its annual cap, Cake Day sends the admin a heads-up. If the cap is hit, the bot skips the celebration and notifies you — skipped celebrations are not retroactively posted on upgrade.
